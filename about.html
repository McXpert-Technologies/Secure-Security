Welcome to Secure Security & Investigations (Pvt) Ltd. We are a team of dedicated professionals who specialize in providing top-notch security solutions to our clients. Secure Security was founded by Mr Sumith Fonseka, an Ex-Commando Regiment Officer in 1996. Ever Since our organization has steadily grown to become an experienced security organization in the island catering to commercial, industrial, residential, and banking sectors of the industry. Our company is committed to delivering the highest levels of safety and protection to individuals, businesses, and organizations across the country.

At our core, we believe that safety is a fundamental human right, and we take it very seriously. Our team comprises highly skilled security professionals who have undergone extensive training in security tactics and techniques. We are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and technology to ensure that we provide the best possible security solutions to our clients.Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We understand that every client has unique security requirements, and we work closely with our clients to develop customized solutions that meet their needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a dynamic leader in national security by providing customer satisfaction through motivated employees, dedicated to excellence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading and most reputed security establishment in the country
