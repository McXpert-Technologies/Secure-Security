About Our Company

Welcome to Secure Security & Investigations (Pvt) Ltd. We are a team of dedicated professionals who specialize in providing top-notch security solutions to our clients. Secure Security was founded by Mr Sumith Fonseka, an Ex-Commando Regiment Officer in 1996. Ever since, our organization has steadily grown to become an experienced security organization in the island, catering to commercial, industrial, residential, and banking sectors of the industry. Our company is committed to delivering the highest levels of safety and protection to individuals, businesses, and organizations across the country.

At our core, we believe that safety is a fundamental human right, and we take it very seriously. Our team comprises of skilled security professionals who have undergone extensive training in security tactics and techniques. We are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and technology to ensure that we provide the best possible security solutions to our clients. Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. We understand that every client has unique security requirements, and we work closely with our clients to develop customized solutions that meet their needs.

Our History

Founded in 1996 by Mr. Sumith Fonseka, a visionary leader in the security industry, this small security company embarked on a remarkable journey. With Mr. Fonseka's expertise and determination, the company gradually expanded its operations, establishing itself as a trusted provider of security services. Over the years, it has evolved to cater to diverse sectors across the country, offering comprehensive security coverage nationwide. Today, this company stands as a testament to Mr. Fonseka's leadership, having successfully built a reputation for excellence and reliability in the field of security services.
